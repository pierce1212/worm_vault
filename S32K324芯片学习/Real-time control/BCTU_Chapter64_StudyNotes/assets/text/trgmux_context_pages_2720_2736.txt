65.1.3 Trigger monitor A trigger monitor is added before the TRGMUX inputs ADC12_0_EOC, ADC12_1_EOC and ADC12_2_EOC. You can configure the UDR registers in SIUL2 to select which trigger needs to propagate to the above TRGMUX inputs. By default after coming out of reset, all the enables are 0. Hence no trigger will be coming to these 3 TRGMUX inputs. TRGMUX Trigger monitor signals Figure 377. Trigger monitor Trigger monitor is not present in S32K314, S32K324 and S32K344.
